Transform-origin :- 
transform-origin is the specified point where the transformation is going to start or applied

transform-origin: center  -->> Expand from the center 
transform-origin: top left  -->> Expand from the top corner left 
transform-origin: bottom right  -->> Expand from the corner bottom right

Translate:-

The main difference betweeen the margin and transform : translate property is that margin will also push your x and y axis direction content and in the case of translate it will overlap your x and y axis content 

transform: translateX(100px)  -->> Move the position of box or container or element in x-axis
transform: translateY(100px) -->> Move the position of box or container or element in y-axis
transform: translate(100px, 100px)  -->> if only translate means the first parameter is for the X-axis and second parameter is for Y-axis
